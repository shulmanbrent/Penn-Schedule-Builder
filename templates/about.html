About our app

Penn Schedule Builder was developed at PennApps XII, in the fall of 2015. Designed to facilitate the process of choosing classes, Penn Schedule Builder takes advantage of the Penn Registrar API in order to deliver schedules tailored to each indiviual's needs.

We are always looking for ways to improve our application, so if you have any comments feel free to reach out!
